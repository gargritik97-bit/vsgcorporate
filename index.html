Who We Are

A Legacy of Financial Excellence

With over two decades of experience, VSG Corporate Services specialises in Project Finance, One-Time Settlements, Corporate Debt Advisory, Working Capital Finance, Non-Fund Based Finance & Structured Funding Solutions. We help businesses access the right capital through our extensive network of banks, financial institutions, and NBFCs across India. We provide end-to-end support, from financial assessment and lender identification to documentation, negotiations, and successful fund disbursement.

What We Do

Comprehensive Financial Services

Project Financing

Long-term infrastructure, industrial projects and public services using non-recourse or limited-recourse financial structures via nationalised and private banks & NBFCs.

  • Preparation of detailed project reports
  • Negotiating best deals on collateral & T&C
  • Term loans for machinery purchase
  • Term loans for unit construction
  • Working capital fund raising

One Time Settlement

We act as mediator between banks and clients to settle Non-Performing Assets (NPAs) at a reduced, mutually beneficial price — resolving financial and legal burdens efficiently.

  • NPA resolution at reduced price
  • Legal & financial burden relief
  • Mediation between bank & borrower
  • Strong team of dedicated professionals
  • Interest of all parties protected

Non-Fund Based Financing

Bank Guarantees and Letters of Credit for government and international private projects — enabling businesses to fulfil obligations without immediate cash outlay.

  • Bank Guarantee (BG) facilitation
  • Letter of Credit (LC) facilitation
  • Government & international projects
  • No direct cash transfer required
  • Secure commitment-based financing

Home Loans

We facilitate home loan solutions for customers purchasing properties in approved residential projects developed by builders. Our team coordinates with banks, builders, and borrowers to ensure a smooth and hassle-free financing experience.

  • Financing for Under-Construction & Ready-to-Move Properties
  • Assistance with Approved Builder Projects
  • Competitive Interest Rates
  • Faster Loan Processing
  • Higher Loan Eligibility Assessment
  • Support with Documentation & Disbursement

CA Suresh Garg

Director & Founder · Chartered Accountant · Insolvency Professional

Suresh Garg is a distinguished Chartered Accountant. Qualified in 1994, with over three decades of professional experience, he has advised corporates, PSUs, banks, and business enterprises on audits, taxation, corporate laws, finance, risk management, and strategic business planning.

An accomplished Insolvency Professional, CA Suresh Garg combines deep financial expertise with practical business insights to deliver effective solutions in corporate finance, debt advisory, restructuring, and regulatory compliance.
